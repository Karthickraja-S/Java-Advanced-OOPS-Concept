# Design a parking system.

## Functional Requirements : 
1. A parking spot can park different vehicles like CAR,BIKE,etc.,
2. A floor can have multiple parking spot
3. A parking lot will have multiple floors.
4. A Lot can have multiple entry & exit gates.
5. Can park and unpack the vehicles.

- Charging :
1. Need to have a charging strategy based on vehicle & hourly basis
   ( Say, if you park evening 7-9 , Parking in EVENING 6-9 Will cost an additional of 20rs per hour)
2. Need to have charging on event basis.
   ( say parking in saturday/sunday costs you 10rs extra from overall amount. ) 
- Payment : 
1. Accepts CASH, CARD , UPI

## Non-Functional Requirements : 
1. A monthly subscription on parking so that charged monthly
2. No more extra charges in the month,
if parked for more than 15 days continuously in a month.
